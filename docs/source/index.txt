meme_get
====================================


This is a library that provides a high-level abstraction for extracting memes from popular online websites. Currently, we support extracting memes from quickmeme.com, memegenerator.net and the Meme subreddit from Reddit.


Authors: Jingnan Shi, Lingdong Huang, Jason Ma
